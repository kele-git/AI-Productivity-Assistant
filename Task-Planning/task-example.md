# Task Planning Feature

## Objective
Demonstrate how AI can assist employees by organizing and prioritizing daily tasks.

## Prompt Used

Act as a productivity assistant.

Create a priority-based task list for the following activities:

- Complete project report
- Attend client meeting
- Reply to emails
- Review budget proposal

Organize the tasks by priority and explain your reasoning.

## AI Output

### High Priority
1. Attend client meeting
   - The meeting is time-sensitive and requires preparation.

2. Complete project report
   - The report is likely linked to deadlines and important deliverables.

### Medium Priority
3. Review budget proposal
   - Financial decisions are important but may not be as urgent as scheduled commitments.

### Low Priority
4. Reply to emails
   - General emails can be addressed after urgent tasks are completed.

## Prompt Engineering Reflection

The prompt specified the role of the AI, the list of tasks, and the expected output format. Asking the AI to explain its reasoning resulted in a more useful and transparent response.
