# Responsible and Ethical Use of AI

This project promotes the responsible use of Artificial Intelligence in the workplace.

## Ethical Considerations

- Confidential workplace information should not be shared with public AI systems.
- AI-generated outputs should always be reviewed and verified by humans.
- Users should be aware of potential bias and inaccuracies in AI responses.
- AI should support human decision-making rather than replace human judgment.
- Organizations should encourage transparency when AI tools are used.

## Conclusion

AI can significantly improve workplace productivity when used responsibly, ethically, and with appropriate human oversight.
