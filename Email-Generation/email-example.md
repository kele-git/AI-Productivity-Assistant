# Email Generation Feature

## Objective
Demonstrate how AI can assist employees by generating professional workplace emails.

## Prompt Used
You are a professional office assistant.

Write a formal email requesting annual leave from 10 July 2026 to 15 July 2026.

Keep the tone professional and concise.

## AI Output

Subject: Annual Leave Request

Dear Manager,

I hope you are well.

I would like to request annual leave from 10 July 2026 to 15 July 2026. I have ensured that my current responsibilities are up to date and will make arrangements for any outstanding tasks before my leave begins.

I would appreciate your consideration of this request and look forward to your response.

Kind regards,

Employee Name

## Prompt Engineering Reflection

The prompt clearly specified:
- The role of the AI (professional office assistant).
- The purpose of the email.
- The dates of leave.
- The desired tone.

Providing these details resulted in a more accurate and professional response.
