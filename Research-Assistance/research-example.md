# Research Assistance Feature

## Objective
Demonstrate how AI can assist employees by summarizing research topics and presenting key information in an easy-to-understand format.

## Prompt Used

Provide a brief summary of Artificial Intelligence in the workplace.

Include the following:
- Benefits
- Challenges
- Common Applications

Keep the explanation suitable for a workplace audience.

## AI Output

### Benefits
- Increases productivity by automating repetitive tasks.
- Improves efficiency and saves time.
- Supports better decision-making through data analysis.

### Challenges
- Data privacy and security concerns.
- Potential bias in AI-generated outputs.
- The need for human oversight and verification.

### Common Applications
- Email automation.
- Virtual assistants and chatbots.
- Meeting summarization.
- Task management and planning.
- Research and information gathering.

## Prompt Engineering Reflection

The prompt clearly defined the topic, the categories to include, and the target audience. This guided the AI to provide a focused and workplace-relevant response rather than a broad overview of AI.
