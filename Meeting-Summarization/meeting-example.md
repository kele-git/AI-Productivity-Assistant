# Meeting Summarization Feature

## Objective
Demonstrate how AI can summarize meeting notes into clear and actionable information.

## Meeting Notes

Weekly Team Meeting

- Project deadline moved to 30 July 2026.
- Sarah will complete the testing phase.
- John will prepare the project documentation.
- The next team meeting is scheduled for Friday.

## Prompt Used

Summarize these meeting notes into the following sections:

1. Key Decisions
2. Action Items
3. Next Meeting

## AI Output

### Key Decisions
- The project deadline has been moved to 30 July 2026.

### Action Items
- Sarah will complete the testing phase.
- John will prepare the project documentation.

### Next Meeting
- Friday.

## Prompt Engineering Reflection

The prompt instructed the AI to organise the information into specific categories. This produced a concise summary that is easier to understand and act upon after a meeting.
