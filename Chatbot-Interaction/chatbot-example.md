# Chatbot Interaction Feature

## Objective
Demonstrate how an AI-powered chatbot can assist employees by answering workplace-related questions and providing guidance.

## Prompt Used

You are an AI workplace productivity assistant.

An employee asks:
"What should I prioritize today if I have the following tasks?"

- Prepare for a client meeting
- Complete a project report due tomorrow
- Review a budget proposal
- Respond to routine emails

Provide a recommended order of priority and explain your reasoning.

## AI Output

Employee Question:
What should I prioritize today?

AI Response:

### Recommended Priority

1. Prepare for the client meeting
   - Meetings are time-sensitive and often require advance preparation.

2. Complete the project report due tomorrow
   - The approaching deadline makes this task urgent.

3. Review the budget proposal
   - Budget reviews are important but can follow urgent commitments.

4. Respond to routine emails
   - Routine emails can be addressed after high-priority responsibilities.

### Additional Advice

Focus on completing the highest-priority tasks first and allocate specific time blocks to avoid multitasking.

## Prompt Engineering Reflection

The prompt defined the AI's role, provided a realistic workplace scenario, and requested both prioritization and reasoning. This resulted in a practical and conversational response that reflects how employees might interact with an AI assistant.
